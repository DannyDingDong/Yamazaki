About Us

We are a traditional authentic family run Japanese Restaurant with over 15 years experienced in industry, crafting the finest Sushi and Sashimi as well as tradtional hot favourites like Rice, Noodles and Soup.

We don't compromise on quality here at Yamazaki, that's why we always use the fresh and finest products to give an excellent experience to our Customers. We always strive to improve ourselves and deliver 5 Star customer service and try to exceed their expectation.
